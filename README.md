# disease_predictor_ml_chatbot
As the name suggests, this chatbot accepts messages from the user containing  their symptoms. After extracting the symptoms from their message, it runs them against the already fed databases - one of diseases and symptoms, and the other of diseases and medical advise. 

Future functionaity - 
1. Make it more general health inclined - ie, now using your age, gender, height, weight and body fat it also guides you on the appropriate steps to take like excercise or calorie management.
2. Add a productivity tracker that computes improvement and efficiency based on calories burnt.
3. For students, a feature similar to (2) except it's based on their study hours and test performance.
